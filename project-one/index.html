Tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr APJ Abdul Kalam

APJ Abdul Kalam, the "The missile man of India." was born on October 15 1931, in Rameshwaram. Tamil Nadu. He serverd as the 11th president of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile programs

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, but his legacy continues to inspire millions.

Achievements

  • Played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Contributed to the development of the SLV-3, India's first satellite launch vehicle.
  • Authored several books, including "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds."
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 1997.
  • Served as a visiting professor at various institutions after his presidency.
